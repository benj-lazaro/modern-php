Text Transformations: Pattern-Based Edits With preg_replace
-----------------------------------------------------------
- The PHP function "preg_replace()"
    - Perform regular expression (text) search and replace

    - Reference (https://www.php.net/manual/en/function.preg-replace.php)


Syntax:

preg_replace(
    string|array $pattern,
    string|array $replacement,
    string|array $subject,
    int $limit = -1,
    int &$count = null
): string|array|null
