Simplifying Constructors: Efficient Initialization & Business Logic Integration
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- To simplify a Constructor declaration in a Class
    - Move the declaration of Properties into the Constructor's parameter list
    - Remove the declaration of Properties

    - It is considered legal to combine the simplified Constructor with
        - Separate declaration of Property
        - Initialize said Property within a Constructor
        - (Optionally) integrate business logic to said Property w/in a Constructor
