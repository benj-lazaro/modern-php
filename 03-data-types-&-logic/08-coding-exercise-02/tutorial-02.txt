Coding Exercise 01: Tutorial Part 02
------------------------------------
- Your task is to refine the digital checkout process for an e-commerce store
    - You will work with two variables
        - $itemName: holds the name of the item being purchased
        - $itemPrice: represents the price of the item in dollars

    - The message should be formatted as follows:

        "Thank you for purchasing the [item]! Your total is $[price]."
